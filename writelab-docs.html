Getting Started

Installation

  1. After downloading WriteLab from writelab.gumroad.com/l/tqdwy, run the installation file
  2. Follow the prompts to complete installation
  3. Launch WriteLab from your desktop or start menu

Your First Resume

When you first open WriteLab, you'll see five tabs at the top of the window:

  • Personal Info: Your basic information and professional summary
  • Education: Your academic history
  • Experience: Your work history
  • Skills: Your abilities organized by category
  • Template: Choose how your resume will look

Use the "Previous" and "Next" buttons at the bottom of the screen to move between sections, or click directly on any tab.

Building Your Resume: Step by Step

Personal Information

This is where your resume begins to take shape. Enter your:

  • Resume title (for your reference)
  • Full name
  • Contact information
  • LinkedIn profile
  • Professional summary (or use our AI assistant to help create one)

TIP: Your professional summary is often the first thing employers read. Make it count by highlighting your unique value and career goals.

Education

Document your educational journey by clicking "Add" and entering:

  • Institution name
  • Degree or certification
  • Dates attended
  • Relevant details or achievements

TIP: List your most recent education first. For established professionals, focus more on your recent experience than older education.

Experience

Your work history tells the story of your career path. For each position:

  • Click "Add" to enter a new position
  • Include company name, title, location, and dates
  • In the description, focus on achievements rather than duties
  • Use action verbs and quantify results when possible

TIP: When describing your experience, think "what did I accomplish?" rather than "what was I responsible for?"

Skills

Showcase what you can do:

  • Choose from predefined categories or use "Other"
  • Add skills relevant to your target positions
  • Organize skills logically by category
  • Remove skills that aren't relevant to your current goals

TIP: Review job descriptions for positions you're targeting and include matching skills that you genuinely possess.

Template

Choose how your resume will look:

  • Professional: Traditional and widely accepted across industries
  • Modern: Contemporary and clean, ideal for forward-thinking companies
  • Academic: Structured format for educational or research positions
  • Creative: Distinctive style for creative fields

TIP: Consider your industry when choosing a template. Some fields favor traditional formats, while others appreciate creative approaches.

The AI Advantage: Your Personal Resume Assistant

WriteLab's AI tools are like having a professional resume writer and career coach by your side.

Generate Professional Summary

Writers block? Let our AI help:

  1. From the menu, select "AI Tools" > "Generate Professional Summary"
  2. Enter details about your experience, skills, and goals
  3. Choose your preferred tone and length
  4. Click "Generate Summary"
  5. Review, edit if needed, and apply to your resume

TIP: Even if you use the AI-generated summary, personalize it to ensure it truly sounds like you.

Generate Cover Letter

Customize a cover letter for each application:

  1. Select "AI Tools" > "Generate Cover Letter"
  2. Enter company information and position details
  3. Paste key requirements from the job description
  4. Explain why you're interested in this specific role
  5. Choose your preferred style
  6. Generate, review, edit if needed, and save

TIP: A great cover letter complements your resume rather than repeating it. Focus on why you're a good fit for this specific role.

ATS Resume Analysis

Ensure your resume gets past automated systems:

  1. Select "AI Tools" > "ATS Resume Analysis"
  2. Paste the job description you're applying for
  3. Select analysis options
  4. Review the detailed feedback
  5. Update your resume based on suggestions

TIP: Many qualified candidates are screened out by ATS systems. This analysis helps ensure your resume includes the right keywords while remaining human-readable.

AI Configuration

To use AI features, you'll need an OpenAI API key:

  1. Select "AI Tools" > "AI Settings"
  2. Enter your OpenAI API key
  3. Select your preferred AI model
  4. Click "OK" to save settings

TIP: You can get an API key from platform.openai.com/api-keys

Saving and Exporting Your Work

Auto-Save

Enable auto-save by checking the "Auto Save" box at the bottom right of the screen to automatically save changes.

Manual Save

  • Click "File" > "Save" to save your current resume
  • Use "File" > "Save As" to create a copy with a new name

Database Management

WriteLab stores your resumes in a local database:

  • "Database" > "List All Resumes" shows your saved resumes
  • Select any resume to load or delete it
  • Search for specific resumes with Ctrl+F

Exporting

When you're ready to send your resume:

  1. Click "Export to PDF" or "Export to Word" (or use File > Export)
  2. Choose a location to save the file
  3. Your professionally formatted resume is ready to share!

TIP: Export to PDF when submitting online to maintain formatting. Export to Word if you need to make further edits or if specifically requested.

Accessibility Features

WriteLab is designed to be accessible to everyone:

  • High Contrast Mode: Improve visibility with better contrast (Accessibility > High Contrast Mode)
  • Larger Text: Increase font size throughout the application (Accessibility > Larger Text)
  • Screen Reader Mode: Optimize the interface for screen readers (Accessibility > Screen Reader Mode)
  • Keyboard Navigation: Use Tab/Shift+Tab to move between fields and Alt+Left/Right for sections

TIP: Press F1 at any time to see keyboard shortcuts and accessibility options.

Keyboard Shortcuts

For efficient navigation and operation:

  • Ctrl+N: New resume
  • Ctrl+O: Open/load resume
  • Ctrl+S: Save resume
  • Ctrl+E: Export menu
  • Alt+Left/Right: Previous/Next section
  • Ctrl+F: Find/search resumes
  • Ctrl+Q: Quit application
  • F1: Show help
  • Alt+H: Toggle high contrast mode
  • Alt+L: Toggle larger text
  • Alt+S: Toggle screen reader mode

Troubleshooting

If WriteLab won't start:

  • Ensure your computer meets the minimum requirements
  • Try reinstalling the application
  • Check for updates through Help > Check for Updates

If changes aren't saving:

  • Check if you have write permissions for the application folder
  • Ensure you have sufficient disk space
  • Try saving to a different location

If AI features aren't working:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Verify your API key is entered correctly in AI Settings
  • Try again after a few minutes

Getting Updates:

  • Select "Help" > "Check for Updates" to see if a new version is available
  • Updates will download and install automatically when available
